About Our

Cool Roof Tiles(CRT)

Our Cool Roof Technology (Tiles) strive to bring innovative products and solutions to the building industry with excellent customer service. Our Cool Roof Technology (Tiles) is the one that has been designed to reflect more sunlight and absorb less heat than a standard cool roof tiles. Nearly any type of building can benefit from a cool roof tiles, but consider the climate and other factors before deciding to install one.

Just as wearing light-colored clothing can keep you cool on a sunny day. Our Tiles add with specialized minerals that are designed to reflect more sunlight and absorb less heat than other roof tiles like terracotta, ceramic etc. Our innovative technologies (Tiles) which help to reduce RCC roof temperature by 5”C -10”C. The product is manufactured to last a life time with a thoughtful balance of new PERLITE TILE TECNOLOGY.
